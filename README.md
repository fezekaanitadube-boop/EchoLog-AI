# EchoLog AI

## Tagline
**Predicting the Ripple Effects of Every Logistics Decision**

## Overview

EchoLog AI is an AI-powered logistics intelligence platform that predicts the chain reaction of operational decisions before they happen. Instead of simply tracking deliveries, EchoLog AI helps organizations understand how one delay or disruption can affect costs, schedules, resources, and customers.

## The Problem

Traditional logistics systems tell you what has already happened.

EchoLog AI predicts what will happen next.

## Core Features

- 🌍 Ripple Impact Engine
- 🧠 AI Decision Simulator
- 🚛 Smart Resource Redistribution
- 📊 Ripple Score
- 📡 Live Logistics Command Center
- 🌦️ Weather Impact Prediction
- ⛽ Fuel Optimization
- 🌱 Carbon Footprint Analysis

## Example

A truck is delayed by 30 minutes.

EchoLog AI predicts:
- Which projects will be delayed
- Estimated financial impact
- Worker idle time
- Equipment downtime
- Customer impact
- Recommended alternative plan
